FAQs

Which containers require a deposit or may be redeemed for a deposit?

If it's carbonated, we'll take it!

All bottles, can, and glass returnables must have a clearly legible barcode or they will not be accepted.

Can I bring beverage containers from other states or countries to Michigan to redeem for deposit?

Short answer: No. If Seinfeld has taught us anything, you'll end up in a mail truck with Newman pursuing your friend Jerry's stolen car.

Are the containers recycled after pick up?

Of course, it's the law.

Do the bottles and cans need to be cleaned before pick up?

Technically a beverage container that contains nothing except the residue of its original contents is acceptable. But if you want to clean them, we won't stop you.

What type of bags should I use to store the bottles in?

Bottles should be packaged in botl bags only. Bags will be provided once you register. If you need extra bags we can bring them to you at each pick-up. Just let us know.
